Tree Profile

Name of Tree

  • Common Name: White Frangipani
  • Scientific Name: Plumeria obtusa

Plumeria obtusa, commonly known as the White Frangipani or Singapore Graveyard Flower, is a small, evergreen tree or shrub native to tropical regions. It is admired for its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers with a yellow center, which bloom year-round in warm climates.

This plant thrives in well-drained soil, full sunlight, and is often used in landscaping and gardens for its ornamental beauty. Plumeria obtusa is drought-tolerant and commonly associated with tropical aesthetics, symbolizing positivity and new beginnings in various cultures.

Location:

This white frangipani tree is located in front of parking section of our college.
